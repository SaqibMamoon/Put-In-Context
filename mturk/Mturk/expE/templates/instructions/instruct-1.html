Instructions - Image Taggging Task

Use ONLY one word to describe what is in the bounding box on the image. The image is in GIF format (a quick flash of series of image frames).
In each GIF image, there are three phases:
Phase 1: You will first see a fixation cross followed by a quick flash of the bounding box with a fixation cross in the center of the bounding box. Pay attention to the bounding box location.
Phase 2: You will see the real image with the region of interest in bounding box. The region of interest will be in the same location as the bounding box shown in Phase 1.
Phase 3: After the real image is presented, a blank grey background is presented.
Phase 4: Try your best to recognize what the object is in the region of interest in the bounding box on the real image as described in Phase 2. Type in your response in the text box. It must be ONLY ONE word, a noun. Please make your best guess. Try not to say things like "I don't know". After this, click "SUBMIT" to go to the next image.
